Pausely

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. About Pausely

Pausely is a parental screen time management app for Android developed by Level X. It helps parents manage and limit their children's screen time by enforcing structured breaks.

2. Information We Collect

Pausely collects only the minimum information necessary to operate the app:

  • Parent email address
  • Child's first name
  • Device name
  • Screen time settings and break preferences
  • Break history, coins, and streak count

Pausely does not collect location data, browsing history, payment information, or any data directly from children.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely to:

  • Operate and deliver the app's core features
  • Send guardian unlock codes via email (Pro feature)
  • Send weekly usage reports to parents (Pro feature)
  • Restore settings if the app is reinstalled

4. COPPA Compliance

Pausely is designed for use by parents and guardians. All information is entered by the parent only. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

5. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on the device using Android SharedPreferences. No personal data is transmitted to external servers except where required for Pro features — EmailJS for guardian emails and RevenueCat for subscription management.

6. Third Party Services

  • RevenueCat — manages Pro subscriptions via Google Play Billing
  • EmailJS — sends guardian unlock code emails and weekly reports (Pro only)
  • Google Play Billing — processes subscription payments

7. Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and is never sold or transferred to third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

8. Your Rights

You may access or delete your data at any time by clearing the app's data in your Android device settings. To request complete data deletion, contact us at the email below.

9. Accessibility

Pausely is committed to accessibility. The app supports Android TalkBack screen reader, meets minimum touch target sizes, and uses sufficient color contrast throughout. To report an accessibility issue contact us at info@levelxinc.com.
