Good to know

Pausely, answered.

What is Pausely?
Pausely is a screen time manager for Android. Parents set a daily limit and a break length; when time's up, the device locks into a full-screen break kids can't skip — with Pip the robot, a healthy activity, and rewards for finishing.
Is Pausely available yet?
Not quite — Pausely is in its final testing phase on Google Play and launching soon. Sign up to be notified the moment it's live.
Who is it for?
Parents and guardians who want to manage a child's screen time. The controls are made for grown-ups and protected by a PIN; the break experience is made to be fun for kids.
Can kids get around the break?
No. The break is a full-screen lock that blocks every app, with the home and back buttons disabled and uninstall protection turned on. Parents lift a break with their PIN, or an emailed code on Pro. Emergency access — phone and WhatsApp buttons on the break screen — is available with Pro.
How much does it cost?
Pausely is free to use, with all the core controls included. An optional Pro upgrade adds emergency buttons and email unlock for $0.99/week, $2.99/month, or $39.99/year, each with a 3-day free trial — or a one-time $79.99 Lifetime unlock with no subscription.
Is my family's data private?
Yes. Pausely stores your data locally on the device rather than in the cloud, and is built to align with COPPA and Google Play Families guidance.
