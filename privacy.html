Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Level X Consulting LLC (“Level X Consulting,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information in connection with our websites and mobile applications, including Pausely (collectively, the “Services”). By using our Services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information we collect

We aim to collect as little personal information as possible. Depending on the app and how you use it, we may collect:

  • Information you provide: such as content you enter into the app (for example, tasks, reminders, or notes), and any messages you send us for support.
  • Device and usage information: such as device type, operating system version, app version, language settings, and basic interaction or crash data used to keep the app working reliably.
  • Purchase information: if you buy a subscription or in-app purchase, the transaction is processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

App data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Services;
  • Save your settings, content, and preferences;
  • Diagnose problems, fix bugs, and improve performance and features;
  • Respond to your support requests; and
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Third-party services

We may use trusted third-party providers to operate our Services. These may include analytics, crash reporting, subscription management (for example, RevenueCat), and the app stores that distribute our apps. These providers process information on our behalf and are bound by their own privacy commitments. We list the specific providers we use here: RevenueCat (subscription management), Google Play (app distribution), and Firebase (remote configuration, used only when parental features are enabled).

4. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only:

  • With service providers who help us operate the Services, as described above;
  • If required by law, regulation, or valid legal process; or
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice to you where required.

5. Children’s privacy

Pausely is designed for use by children and families. We take children’s privacy seriously and comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the Google Play Families policy. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction) without verifiable parental consent. The app stores data locally on the device and does not transmit personal information to our servers. We do not serve behavioral advertising, and any third-party services used in the app comply with applicable child-safety requirements. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us at the email below so we can promptly remove it.

6. Data retention

We keep information only as long as needed to provide the Services and for legitimate business or legal purposes. You can delete app content on your device at any time, and you may request deletion of any data we hold by contacting us.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below and we will respond as required by applicable law.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information against loss, misuse, and unauthorized access. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version here and revise the “Last updated” date above. Material changes will be communicated through the app or our website where appropriate.
